Welcome, Geometry Explorers!

This interactive toolkit is designed to help you see and experience the geometry concepts we're learning in class. Each module below lets you drag, resize, and manipulate geometric figures to discover relationships on your own. Don't just memorize — explore, observe, and understand why these properties are always true.

Every module has a 📖 Guided Lesson button if you need a walkthrough, and a 📸 Screenshot button to capture your work. Jump into any module to get started!

📚 Exploration Modules
🔺
Module 1
Similar Triangles
Define a reference triangle, then drag and manipulate a second triangle to explore similarity. Watch corresponding angles, side ratios, and similarity postulates update in real time.
Draggable AA / SAS / SSS Transformations
⬡
Module 2
Midsegments
Toggle midsegments on and off to discover the Midsegment Theorem. See how three midsegments divide a triangle into four congruent triangles, each similar to the original.
Draggable 1:2 Ratios 4 Congruent △s
📐
Module 3
Trig Ratios — SOHCAHTOA
Explore right triangle trigonometry by dragging vertices and watching sine, cosine, and tangent update live. Lock an angle and resize to prove the ratios stay constant.
Sin / Cos / Tan Angle Lock Practice Mode
⭐
Module 4
Special Right Triangles
Investigate 45-45-90 and 30-60-90 triangles with draggable figures, input practice, and radical/decimal toggling. Explore Pythagorean Triple families and verify triples.
45-45-90 30-60-90 Pyth. Triples
🔵
Module 5
Unit Circle Connection
See how the trig ratios from right triangles connect to the unit circle. Drag a point around the circle and watch sin and cos come to life as coordinates.
Interactive Sin & Cos Visual Link
